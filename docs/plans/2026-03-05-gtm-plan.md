# GTM Plan: AI Store Secretary

**Date:** March 2026 | **Status:** Draft | **Purpose:** Go-to-market strategy cho launch

---

## 1. Positioning Statement

**For** solo Shopify merchants ($5K-$50K/month) dang dung 5-10 apps roi rac,
**who** kiet suc voi viec quan ly store mot minh,
**AI Store Secretary is** mot doi ngu AI agents phoi hop quan ly store operations,
**that** thay the nhieu apps bang mot platform thong nhat — agents share context va tu phoi hop hanh dong,
**unlike** Sidekick (broad, shallow) va point solutions (deep nhung isolated),
**we** cung cap lop orchestration thong minh — merchant compose workflow rieng tu doi agent mo rong lien tuc.

**One-line pitch:** "Shopify store cua ban gio la cong ty 2 nguoi. Ban la sep. Chung toi la Secretary."

---

## 2. Launch Strategy: 3 Giai doan (Bootstrapped → Reinvest)

| Giai doan | Thoi gian | Muc tieu | Budget |
|-----------|-----------|----------|--------|
| **Phase 1: Seed** | Thang 1-2 | 100 merchants dau tien, validate PMF | $0 (organic only) |
| **Phase 2: Grow** | Thang 3-6 | 1,000 merchants, optimize funnel | Reinvest revenue |
| **Phase 3: Scale** | Thang 6-12 | 5,000-10,000 merchants, platform narrative | Revenue + co the raise |

## 3. Phase 1: Seed (Thang 1-2, Budget $0)

### Muc tieu
- 100 installs
- 10-15 paying merchants
- Validate: merchant co quay lai doc briefing hang ngay khong?

### Channels

#### 3.1 Shopify App Store (Kenh #1 — 60-70% installs)

**Tai sao:** Day la noi merchants tim apps. SEO/ASO tot = traffic mien phi lien tuc.

**Actions:**
- Toi uu listing: title co "AI", "store management", "automation"
- Screenshots: before/after dashboard, morning briefing demo
- App description: focus "thay the 5 apps" narrative
- Keywords: "AI store manager", "store automation", "AI secretary", "store optimization"
- Demo video: 60 giay — cho thay morning briefing + agent findings
- Reviews: xin 5-10 beta users danh gia 5 sao ngay tuan dau

**KPI:** 200-500 listing views/week → 50-100 installs/month

#### 3.2 Shopify Community Forums

**Tai sao:** Merchants hoi cau hoi o day. Tra loi co gia tri = trust + organic installs.

**Actions:**
- Theo doi topics: "too many apps", "store management", "AI tools", "automation"
- Tra loi that, co gia tri — khong spam link
- Share insights tu product (anonymized) — "chung toi phat hien 60% stores co broken schema"
- Soft CTA: "chung toi dang build tool giai quyet van de nay, check it out"

**KPI:** 5-10 installs/week

#### 3.3 Reddit (r/shopify, r/ecommerce, r/entrepreneur)

**Tai sao:** Community lon, merchant that, cho phep self-promotion neu co gia tri.

**Actions:**
- Post kieu "build in public": "Chung toi build AI Secretary cho Shopify — day la nhung gi hoc duoc"
- Share data/insights that: "Phan tich 100 Shopify stores — 5 loi pho bien nhat"
- AMA (Ask Me Anything) ve AI trong e-commerce
- Comment co gia tri tren posts lien quan

**KPI:** 3-5 installs/week, 1-2 viral posts/month

#### 3.4 Twitter/X — Build in Public

**Tai sao:** Long-term audience building. Investor cung doc Twitter.

**Actions:**
- Daily thread: metrics, learnings, merchant stories
- Show product development: screenshots, demos, agent outputs
- Engage voi Shopify developer community
- Tag @Shopify, @ShopifyDevs khi relevant

**KPI:** 500 followers trong 2 thang, 2-3 installs/week

#### 3.5 YouTube (2-3 videos)

**Tai sao:** Evergreen content. Merchants search YouTube truoc khi install apps.

**Actions:**
- Video 1: "AI Store Secretary — Demo 5 phut" (product overview)
- Video 2: "Toi thay the 5 Shopify apps bang 1 AI Secretary" (use case story)
- Video 3: "Morning Briefing: AI quan ly store cua ban nhu the nao" (feature deep dive)

**KPI:** 500-1,000 views/video trong 2 thang, 5-10 installs/video

## 4. Phase 2: Grow (Thang 3-6, Reinvest Revenue)

### Muc tieu
- 1,000 installs
- 100-150 paying merchants
- Free-to-Paid conversion > 10%
- Churn < 12%/month

### Channels moi

#### 4.1 Content Marketing (Blog)

**Format:** Merchant-facing blog tren domain rieng hoac Shopify blog.

**Content pillars:**
- "How to" guides: "How to automate store management with AI"
- Case studies: "Merchant X tiet kiem 10 gio/tuan voi AI Secretary"
- Data insights: "Phan tich 500 Shopify stores: top 5 van de storefront"
- Comparison posts: "AI Secretary vs Vitals vs 5 apps rieng le"

**Frequency:** 2 bai/tuan

#### 4.2 Shopify Partner Network

**Tai sao:** Agencies va freelancers recommend apps cho clients. 1 partner = nhieu merchants.

**Actions:**
- Lien he 20-30 Shopify agencies nho
- Offer: affiliate commission 20% recurring hoac partner pricing
- Cung cap assets: demo account, pitch deck, case studies
- Co-marketing: guest blog, joint webinar

**KPI:** 10 active partners → 50-100 installs/month

#### 4.3 Product Hunt Launch

**Tai sao:** 1 ngay co the mang 500-2,000 signups. Investor cung theo doi PH.

**Preparation:**
- 2 tuan truoc: build hunter network, prepare assets
- Launch day: coordinate upvotes tu community, respond moi comment
- Target: Top 5 Product of the Day

**KPI:** 500-1,000 signups trong 1 tuan

#### 4.4 Email Nurture (Free → Paid)

**Drip sequence cho free users:**
- Day 1: Welcome + setup guide
- Day 3: "Day la nhung gi Secretary phat hien cho store cua ban" (first briefing highlight)
- Day 7: "Ban dang miss nhung insights nay" (teaser premium features)
- Day 14: "Merchants nhu ban tiet kiem X gio/tuan" (social proof + upgrade CTA)
- Day 21: "Thu Autopilot mode mien phi 7 ngay" (trial offer)

**KPI:** Email open rate > 40%, Free→Paid conversion 10-15%

#### 4.5 Referral Program

**Co che:** Merchant moi merchant — ca 2 duoc 1 thang free (hoac 1 thang upgrade tier).

**KPI:** 15-20% new installs tu referral

## 5. Phase 3: Scale (Thang 6-12)

### Muc tieu
- 5,000-10,000 installs
- 500-1,000 paying merchants
- ARR: $300K-$720K
- Platform narrative established

### Channels moi

#### 5.1 Agent Marketplace Announcement

**Narrative:** "Shopify's first AI agent platform — build agents, not apps"

**Actions:**
- PR push: TechCrunch, Shopify blog, e-commerce publications
- Developer outreach: "Build agents cho AI Store Secretary"
- Launch 2-3 third-party agents de chung minh concept

#### 5.2 Integrations & Partnerships

**Muc tieu:** Cross-promote voi cac apps merchant da dung.

**Partners tiem nang:**
- Judge.me → Review data feed cho Review Agent
- Klaviyo → Email recommendations tu agent insights
- Inventory Planner → Data sharing 2 chieu

#### 5.3 Paid Acquisition (neu unit economics proven)

**Dieu kien:** LTV/CAC > 3x, payback period < 6 thang

**Channels:**
- Google Ads: "Shopify AI", "store automation", "AI store manager"
- Facebook/Instagram retargeting: merchants da visit listing
- Budget: $5K-$10K/month, bat dau $50/day va scale

#### 5.4 Conferences & Events

- Shopify Unite / Editions
- E-commerce expos
- AI/SaaS conferences (SaaStr, etc.)
- Demo booth + live merchant stories

## 7. Competitive Response Plan

### Kich ban 1: Sidekick mo rong storefront intelligence
- **Response:** Da co merchant habits + deeper domain expertise
- **Messaging pivot:** "Specialist vs generalist" — "Sidekick biet moi thu, Secretary gioi moi thu"
- **Action:** Accelerate Autopilot mode — tao switching cost cao truoc khi Sidekick bat kip

### Kich ban 2: Point solution copy multi-agent approach
- **Response:** Orchestration layer la 6-12 thang ahead
- **Action:** Ship agents nhanh hon, focus platform story, open agent SDK cho developers

### Kich ban 3: New AI-native competitor xuat hien
- **Response:** First-mover advantage + merchant base + community
- **Action:** Speed of execution + customer love + referral moat

### Kich ban 4: Merchant khong adopt AI agents (thi truong cham)
- **Response:** Focus Advisor mode — gia tri nhu "smart dashboard" khong can trust AI
- **Action:** Double down tren content marketing va education

## 8. Unit Economics Muc tieu (Year 1)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| CAC (Organic) | < $10 |
| CAC (Paid, Phase 3) | < $50 |
| Blended ARPU | $50/month |
| Gross margin | > 70% (sau AI API costs) |
| LTV (12-month) | $400-$500 |
| LTV/CAC | > 10x (organic), > 8x (paid) |
| Payback period | < 1 month (organic), < 3 months (paid) |

**AI cost estimate per merchant:**
- ~$5-$10/month/merchant (Claude API calls cho 5 agents, daily runs)
- Giam theo thoi gian: prompt optimization, caching, batch processing
- Gross margin healthy ngay tu dau vi ARPU > AI cost

## 9. Pricing Strategy

| Tier | Gia | Bao gom | Muc tieu |
|------|-----|---------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | 2 agents, 10 products, Advisor mode | Top-of-funnel, PMF validation |
| **Starter** | $29/mo | All agents, 100 products, Advisor mode | Solo merchants moi bat dau |
| **Pro** | $79/mo | All agents, unlimited products, Assistant mode | Core revenue tier |
| **Agency** | $199/mo | Multi-store (10), white-label, Autopilot mode | Agencies, power users |

**Value anchor:** Merchants dang tra $50-$150/month cho 5 apps roi rac. Secretary $79/month thay the tat ca = tiet kiem + tot hon.

**Pricing psychology:** "Thue 1 nhan vien AI full-time chi $79/month" — frame nhu HR cost, khong phai SaaS cost.
